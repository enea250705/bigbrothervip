## How to Submit to Google

### Step 1: Submit Sitemap
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Add your property (website URL)
3. Navigate to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
4. Add: `https://www.bigbrothervipalbania.com/sitemap.xml`
5. Click "Submit"

### Step 2: Verify Ownership
- Upload HTML file to your server, OR
- Add meta tag to your HTML, OR
- Use Google Analytics, OR
- Use DNS verification

### Step 3: Request Indexing
1. In Google Search Console, go to "URL Inspection"
2. Enter your homepage URL
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. Repeat for important pages

### Step 4: Submit to Other Search Engines
- **Bing Webmaster Tools**: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
- **Yandex Webmaster**: https://webmaster.yandex.com
- **Baidu Webmaster**: https://ziyuan.baidu.com (for Chinese market)
